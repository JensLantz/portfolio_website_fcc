About Me

  • Name: Jens Lantz
  • Age: 23
  • Occupation: Web Developer

I would describe myself as a native techie, meaning that I found my passion for computers and technology very early; it might also be a result of my gadget inclined father and internet enlightened brothers. At the moment, I’m finishing my Master's degree in Information Systems at Lund University; thereafter, I’m on the hunt for an exceptional talent program where I can challenge myself within the realms of software development to acquire new skills among senior professionals and likeminded talents. Throughout my education and growth as a developer I’ve learnt many languages, such as Java, C#, JavaScript, Python, SQL, and many surrounding technologies, such as Git, Microsoft SQL Server, and Java EE. With this constant new exposure, I’ve come to realize that the most important skill I possess is being able to learn quickly and intuitively.

This conceptual view is something I use in environments other than software development too; for example, I am well versed in modeling languages such as UML, ER, and BPMN, and have used these languages extensively during full stack system designs and implementations in mock cases during the course of my education.

To round things off, besides software development some of my interests include:

  • 1) Board games, with Sequence being a personal favorite.
  • 2) Music production, where I'm drawn to the modular design of Ableton.
  • 3) Weightlifting, to make sure I don't become one with my office chair.
